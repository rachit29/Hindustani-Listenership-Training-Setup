Ektal Rhythm Training Videos for Group 2

Ektal is another tala of Hindustani music.

It has a 12-beat pattern.

>>>>> Here are the videos of Ektal with beat pattern represented in a cycle. Please try to follow the beat pattern and understand its structure.

IMPORTANT: Keep in mind that the goal is to find the "Sam" which is represented by "Dhin" on the top of rhythm circle. Try to learn the timing of the repeating "Sam".
