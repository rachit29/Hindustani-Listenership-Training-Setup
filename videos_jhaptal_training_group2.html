Jhaptal Rhythm Training Videos for Group 2

Jhaptal is another tala of Hindustani music. It presents quite a different rhythmical structure from Tintal (which you heard in the first session), unlike which it is not symmetrical.

Jhaptal has a 10-beat pattern. It has ten beats in four divisions, of 2-3-2-3, the third of which is the khali, or open division.

>>>>> Here are the videos of Jhaptal with beat pattern represented in a cycle. Please try to follow the beat pattern and understand its structure.

IMPORTANT: Keep in mind that the goal is to find the "Sam" which is represented by "Dhin" on the top of rhythm circle. Try to learn the timing of the repeating "Sam".
