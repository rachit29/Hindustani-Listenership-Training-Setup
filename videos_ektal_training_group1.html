Ektal Rhythm Training Videos for Group 1

Ektal is another tala of Hindustani music.

It has a 12-beat pattern.

>>>>> To count the Ektal,try to mimic the hand gestures in the following videos.

To follow the tal: Clap on the appropriate beat, which in EKtal is beats 1, 5, 9 and 11. A wave of the hand at beat 3 and 7.

IMPORTANT: The tal is divided into pattern of 2+2+2+2+2, however in this tal there are subdivisions in the beat pattern represented by "ge and ra,ki,tÌ£a" which you can see on the Circle. These sub divisions make this beat sound distinct, its important to identify them to find the "Sam" and also to recognise the "Tal"
