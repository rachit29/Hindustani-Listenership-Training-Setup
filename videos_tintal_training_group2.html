Tintal Rhythm Training Videos for Group 2

Tintal (or teental, trital; Hindi: तीन ताल) is the most common tala of Hindustani music. Tintal has sixteen (16) beats in four equal divisions. The first beat out of 16 beats is called sam and the 9th beat is called khali ('empty').

IMPORTANT: Keep in mind that the goal is to find the "Sam" which is represented by "Dha" on the top of rhythm circle. Try to learn the timing of the repeating "Sam".
